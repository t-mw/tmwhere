Talking Heads: Marketing & Design

TUES

March 3 / 18:00-20:00 / Wee Red Bar, Edinburgh College of Art

We’re teaming up with Creative Edinburgh for a fast paced evening of lightning talks and informal networking; featuring 10 guest speakers including Designers, Makers, illustrators, App Developers and more. Open to industry, as well as students, join us for an evening of inspiring stories, practical tips and leave with new contacts, opportunities and ideas. Please note that you must book onto this event. Places are limited, please book your space here: https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/creative-cultural-careers-festival-talking-heads-tickets-15634444043
