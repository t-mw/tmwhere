Get Spotted

FRI

March 6 / 14:00-15:00 / LG07, David Hume Tower

Interactive workshop. Want to know how you can get your work spotted by galleries and buyers? Come and meet Art Retail Network who will give you tips on how to present yourself to galleries, advice on how to get social media exposure and inform you how to get noticed by industry professionals. Bring your laptops or tablets to this interactive session.

Please note that you must book onto this event. Places are limited, please book your space here: https://mycareerhub.ed.ac.uk/students/events/detail/228210


Speaker

Art Retail Network provides an online service match-making artists with galleries scouting for new talent. ARN believes for every artistic creation there is someone somewhere who will pay a fair price for it. But all too often they never find each other. Our mission is to help make connections happen. ARN are currently running the Spotted campaign to discover new artists across the UK.
