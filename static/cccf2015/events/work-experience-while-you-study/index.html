Work Experience While You Study

THURS

March 5 / 13:15-14:15 / Level 1 Mezzanine, Library

Speed Networking. Edinburgh has a huge range of volunteering opportunities for students and it is a great way to add to your CV while you study. Come along to this event and explore some of these opportunities across the Creative and Cultural sectors. Spaces are limited and numbers will be allocated on arrival. If you have an accessibility enquiry please get in touch with employers@ed.ac.uk by Tuesday 3rd March.
