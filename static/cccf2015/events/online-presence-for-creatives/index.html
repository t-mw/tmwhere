Online Presence for Creatives

TUES

March 3 / 11:00-12:00 / O17 - Hunter Lecture Theatre, Hunter Building, Edinburgh College of Art

Interactive Workshop. This interactive workshop will cover top tips for promoting your talent online and building your network through social media, blogging and online portfolio. Students are recommended to bring their laptops or tablets.


Speaker

Colin Gilchrist, Social Tailor, Brand Strategy and Content Producer, Digital Marketing and Media Manager.
