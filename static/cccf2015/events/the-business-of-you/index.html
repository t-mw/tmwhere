The Business of You

FRI

March 6 / 10:00-11:15 / LG11 David Hume Tower

Interactive Workshop. Are you interested in creating a career out of your talent? This practical session will focus on you, your ideas and values and how you can translate these and your own practical skills and experience into action and build a creative enterprise of your own.

Please note that you must book onto this event. Places are limited, please book your space here: https://mycareerhub.ed.ac.uk/students/events/detail/228735/creative-and-cultural-careers-


Speaker

Libby Anson is the Student Employability and Enterprise Manager at Glasgow School of Art. She trained as a fine artist and has worked in the public and private gallery sectors as a curator and educator and she continues to be a freelance arts writer and editor. She has been an HE lecturer and independent coach in creative and professional practice since 1994 and has wide experience of supporting students, graduates and practising visual artists, designers and writers with their personal and career development.
