Since seeing the city generation from the shelved ??? game ??? in action, I've wanted to to try writing a basic procedural city generator myself. The developers for ??? followed a method described in Parish and Müller's paper: Procedural Modelling of Cities (200?). The paper is well-written and accessible, besides the authors' decision to define their algorithm as an L-system. The rules defined for the system are hard to interpret for a layperson and, as argued by one forum thread author, can be transformed into an equivalent but more familiar form. The demonstration above is the result of implementing this form of the algorithm with some of the 'global' and 'local goals' suggested by Parish and Müller.
